COVID-19 RESSOURCES

#StaySafe

We take this opportunity to share with you some useful information to face the COVID-19 world crisis. Personal protective equipment (PPE) is a worker’s safety net to avoid possible injury or death. It eliminates most workplace hazard. Companies have a responsibility to procure the needed equipment for their team. Synergie Canada would like to encourage companies to embrace the new security measures and for everyone to #staysafe!

Synergie Canada has built solid expertise in project cargo throughout the years, our team can assist you importing delicate merchandise such as PPE during the COVID-19 world crisis. There are a lot of things to take into account when it comes to transport high-value, critical merchandise, please entrust your goods to a team of professionals.
